Our Story

A Dream That Started Small

Dreamy Cream began in 2010 as a small ice cream cart in Central Park. Founder Molly Dreamer wanted to create ice cream that would transport people to their happiest memories with every bite.

Today, we have 5 locations across the city, but we still make our ice cream the same way - small batches with big love.

Crafted With Care

We source only the finest ingredients - organic dairy from local farms, seasonal fruits at their peak ripeness, and premium chocolates and nuts.

Each flavor is developed over months of testing to create the perfect balance of taste and texture. Our slow-churned process creates an ultra-creamy texture that's simply dreamy.

Meet the Dream Team

Our team of "dream weavers" includes master ice cream makers, flavor scientists, and happiness ambassadors who ensure every visit to Dreamy Cream is magical.

From our scoopers who remember regulars' favorite flavors to our delivery team that gets your ice cream to you at the perfect temperature - we all share a passion for creating sweet dreams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer dairy-free options?

Yes! We have a rotating selection of delicious dairy-free flavors made with coconut milk, almond milk, or oat milk bases. These are made in a separate facility to avoid cross-contamination.

What are your COVID safety measures?

All staff are vaccinated and wear masks. We've installed plexiglass barriers at ordering stations and provide hand sanitizer throughout our shops. Online ordering with curbside pickup is available at all locations.

Can I order a custom ice cream cake?

Absolutely! We require 72 hours notice for custom cakes. You can choose any combination of our flavors and toppings. Visit our contact page to start your order.

Do you ship nationwide?

We currently ship to all 50 states via overnight delivery with special dry ice packaging to ensure your ice cream arrives frozen and perfect.
